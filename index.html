-About us-

E-soko is a multi-vendor E-commerce website. It was founded in 2020. The idea was fueled by the Covid-19 pandemic. Due to this, the ordinary marketplaces could not be trusted since fruits and vegetables would go for days without being sold and hence people would buy stale products.We noticed that fruits and vegetables were the most essential needs for consumers to maintain a healthy body. Due to the lock-down put in place, producers reported losses since retailers could not get to the markets to buy the products they needed in their groceries. E-soko was created to connect the producers, consumers and retailers virtually and this could also give jobs to riders or transporters. We say "farm to table" since the platform enables retailers and consumers to place their orders and also gives the producers a platform to showcase their products without necessarily bringing them to the market. We pride ourselves on efficiency and are dedicated to our customers needs. With E-soko, there is something for everyone.
